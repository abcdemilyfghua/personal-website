Love Island USA Season 8 just recently came out and it's been all that people are talking about. My social media feeds are filled with it. I was walking my dog yesterday and two people walked by talking about "Sincere and Amora" and spilling their thoughts about what had, I assume, just happened on screen. Normally, FOMO gets to me good and I get aboard the on-time train to watch Love Island. This summer, though, I was fine being late. 

I first heard about the release of the show when hanging out with a friend in San Francisco. We were headed to a poker night social at some startup with some of his fellow interns and it was **the** topic to talk about. They were all planning to watch it together. *so cute lol* Anyway, that was weeks and weeks ago. I kind of forgot about it and nothing in me was dying to watch it. 

The other day, my sister and I were eating dinner together. It was late, maybe 10pm, and we had just finished doing [some renovations](/blog/adult-lego) for her room. It had been a long day. 

i dont think my life would ever get so bad to the point where i try to find smo to date on a reality show
